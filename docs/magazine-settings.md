# HER MAGAZINE Magazine Settings

## Concept

HER MAGAZINE is a private fashion-magazine-style heroine diagnosis app for iPhone. It should feel like opening a small personal cover feature, not a task tracker or self-improvement tool.

## Magazine Identity

- Magazine name: HER MAGAZINE
- Tagline: 今日もあなたが主役です。
- Issue style: Daily private fashion issue
- Tone: gentle, polished, cute, light, affirming
- Avoid: pressure, goals, follower metrics, heavy encouragement

## Visual Direction

- iPhone-first cover layout
- Large but readable masthead
- Intentional line breaks only
- Editorial labels such as `Today's Issue`, `Cover Feature`, `Lucky Item`
- Soft fashion colors with rose, ivory, champagne, lavender, and muted charcoal
- Result screen should feel like a magazine cover, with the date and lucky item included in the cover composition

## Copy Rules

- Use short lines that fit narrow screens
- Keep Japanese text warm and light
- Use fashion-magazine phrasing without making the user feel evaluated
- Keep the user as the subject of the cover
